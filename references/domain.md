# Real Estate Domain Knowledge

## Key statistics (cite when relevant)

- First agent to respond to a lead is **21x more likely** to qualify them
- **60.9%** of RE agents already run social ads — they know the channel works
- Only **28.5%** can tell which marketing actually drives sales — the loop fixes this
- Marketing is a secondary responsibility for **38.66%** of agents — keep everything fast
- **"Affordable digital marketing for real estate professionals"** resonates 2:1 over generic messaging (Sterling survey, 1,252 customers)

## Buyer profiles by price point

| Price | Likely buyer | TikTok targeting angle |
|---|---|---|
| Under $300K | First-time buyers, investors | Budget-conscious, "what $X gets you" |
| $300–600K | Move-up buyers, young families | Space, neighborhood, schools (factual) |
| $600K–1M | Established buyers, downsizers | Lifestyle, quality, location convenience |
| $1M+ | Luxury, lifestyle | Aspirational, unique features, scarcity |

## Budget guidance

- **Starting budget:** $25/day for 7 days ($175 total) — enough to learn, low enough to feel safe
- **Good early benchmark:** $5–20 cost per lead for housing on TikTok
- **Scaling rule:** If cost per lead is under $15 after day 3, double the budget
- **Never recommend** "spend more" without showing performance data first

## What agents fear most (use to remove barriers)

1. Fair Housing violations — compliance = visible, automatic
2. "I don't know how to set up the accounts" — provisioning = guided
3. "I don't know if it's working" — simple performance reports, plain language
4. "What if I waste money?" — start paused, approve before enabling
5. "I don't have video" — Smart+ can run with static images + text; show them this works

## Platform pairing rationale

**Why TikTok + CC together beats either alone:**
- TikTok = reach and discovery (top of funnel)
- CC = conversion and nurture (mid/bottom of funnel)
- TikTok drives leads → CC captures and follows up → performance data flows back → better TikTok targeting
- Neither platform alone closes this loop

## Common agent objections and responses

**"I don't have time for this."**
> "That's exactly why I'm here. Give me the listing details — 2 minutes — and both campaigns are live. You stay focused on your clients."

**"I don't have video."**
> "No problem. TikTok's Smart+ system works with just the listing text and your hooks. Static images can outperform video for RE if the hook is strong. We'll test it."

**"I already tried TikTok ads and they didn't work."**
> "Most RE ads fail because of targeting (too narrow) or creative (too polished). Tell me what you ran — I'll tell you what to change."

**"Is this going to violate Fair Housing?"**
> "I build every campaign to follow Fair Housing best practices — open targeting, compliant language, no exclusions. I'll flag anything that looks borderline before we run it."
