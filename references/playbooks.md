# The Playbooks

This skill does two things: launch a coordinated TikTok ad + email campaign from a listing, and report on how it performed. Everything else is a different skill.

---

## Playbook 1: Listing Launch

**When:** Agent has a new listing and wants to reach buyers fast.

**The sequence:**
1. Gather listing data (MLS or pasted text)
2. Identify ideal buyer from price + property type:
   - <$300K: first-time buyers, investors
   - $300–600K: move-up buyers, families
   - $600K–1M: established buyers, downsizers
   - $1M+: luxury, lifestyle buyers
3. Generate 3 hooks (one per formula: reveal, price anchor, FOMO)
4. Create TikTok Upgraded Smart+ campaign (paused)
5. Create CC email blast (draft, link to editor)
6. Deliver both with clear next steps

**Timing intelligence:**
- Spring (Mar–May): Go aggressive — peak season, maximum inventory
- Summer (Jun–Aug): Lifestyle content, neighborhood focus
- September: "RE new year" — activity picks back up
- Fall/Winter: Motivated buyers, less competition, emphasize urgency

**Content that works on TikTok for RE:**
- Property reveals ("Wait till you see...") — curiosity, room-by-room
- Price anchoring ("What $X gets you in [city]") — context, works in any market
- Neighborhood tours ("3 things I love about [area]") — builds agent brand
- Before/after — staging transformations, renovation reveals
- Day-in-the-life — agent personality content builds trust over time

**Email subject line formulas:**
- "New listing: [X]bd/[Y]ba in [neighborhood], $[price]"
- "Just listed — [standout feature] in [city]"
- "[Price] in [area]? See it before Sunday."
- "Your buyers are looking for this."

## Playbook 2: The Performance Loop

**When:** Campaign has been running 3+ days, time to analyze and improve.

**The loop:** Data from campaign 1 improves targeting for campaign 2. This is the moat — neither TikTok nor CC alone can do cross-channel attribution.

**What to surface:**
1. Which hook won? (video view rate per ad variant)
2. What's the cost per lead? (benchmark: $5–20 for RE on TikTok)
3. Email engagement by contact segment (opens, clicks, which listing resonated)
4. Cross-channel: did people who opened the email also engage with the TikTok ad?
5. Lead quality signal: how many showings from this campaign?

**How to present:**
Simple. Vanity metrics first (reach, opens), then actionable (cost per lead, which hook won). One recommendation for next time.

Example:
> "Your Elm St campaign reached 14,000 people. The 'kitchen reveal' hook drove 3x more leads than the price hook — your audience responds to features over price. For your next listing, lead with the standout room. Also: 47 contacts opened the email but didn't click. Want me to retarget them on TikTok?"
